About Konst

Konst is an initiative by D'Coders Club - The Techninal Institute Of SRMGPC.

It is aimed at spreading the knowledge of computer science and programming and Konst is an integral part of that goal. It aims to challege computer science students with simple yet unique questions that require "out of the box" thinking and quick problem solving.

Konst is 100% free-to-use and offers certifications free of cost to the top performers.
